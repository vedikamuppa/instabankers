PERSONAL LOAN

Personal Loan – Interest Rates, Eligibility, Features

A personal loan is a loan availed by individuals to meet their financial commitments or requirements. This type of loan comes in handy if you have unexpected expenses to take care of. Individuals use the funds received through a personal loan to make big purchases, consolidate high-interest debts, meet wedding expenses and educational expenses,  go on a trip, and many other reasons.

Features and Benefits of Personal Loans

  • Personal loans are unsecured loans that do not require the applicant to pledge any asset as collateral.
  • Personal loans are provided based on the borrower’s creditworthiness analysed based on their credit report/history.
  • In the case of a home loan or car loan, you can use the funds only to purchase a house or a car. Funds from a personal loan can be used for any purpose.
  • Loan tenure can range from 12 months up to 60 months based on your convenience.
  • inimal documentation, such as proof of identity, address, and income, is enough to apply for a personal loan.
